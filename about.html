Our Story

Welcome to Auric, where traditional craftsmanship meets contemporary design to create timeless pieces of luxury jewelry.

Priya Malhotra

Founder & Lead Designer

With over 15 years of experience in the jewelry industry, Priya Malhotra founded Auric with a vision to create exquisite pieces that celebrate India's rich heritage of jewelry craftsmanship while embracing modern aesthetics.

Her passion for traditional techniques and dedication to sourcing the finest materials has established Auric as a premier destination for luxury jewelry that tells a story and creates lasting impressions.

Every Auric piece begins with Priya's hand-drawn designs, inspired by India's architectural marvels, cultural motifs, and natural beauty, transformed by skilled artisans into wearable art.

Our Expertise

Designer Gold

Our gold jewelry combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibilities. Each piece undergoes meticulous crafting using 22K and 18K gold, ensuring exceptional quality and lasting beauty.

Diamond Jewelry

We source only the finest diamonds for our collections, each stone meeting strict criteria for cut, clarity, color, and carat. Our diamond pieces are designed to capture light and attention with unparalleled brilliance.

Polki Creations

Our polki pieces celebrate the ancient art of uncut diamond setting, a traditional Indian technique. These unique creations highlight the natural beauty of uncut diamonds in contemporary designs.
